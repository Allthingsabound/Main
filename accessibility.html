Accessibility Statement

All Things Abound is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone.

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

Our Commitment

All Things Abound is committed to making our website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

We believe that all people, regardless of disability, should have equal access to information and services online. This commitment guides our design and development decisions.

Conformance Status

We are working toward full conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Our current website incorporates the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML: We use proper HTML5 semantic elements (headings, landmarks, lists) to provide meaningful structure
  • ARIA Labels: Navigation, sections, and interactive elements include appropriate ARIA roles and labels
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard without requiring a mouse
  • Color Contrast: We aim to maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text
  • Alt Text: All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text
  • Responsive Design: The website is responsive and functional across different screen sizes and orientations
  • Focus Indicators: Visible focus indicators are provided for keyboard users
  • Skip Navigation: A skip-to-main-content link is available for screen reader and keyboard users
  • Text Resizing: Content remains readable and functional when text is resized up to 200%
  • No Autoplay: We do not use audio or video that plays automatically

Technical Specifications

All Things Abound’s website relies on the following technologies for conformance with WCAG 2.1:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript (WAI-ARIA when applicable)

These technologies are relied upon for conformance. The website has been tested with major modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge that there may be some areas that do not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We are actively working to identify and address these issues. Known areas we are improving include:

  • Enhanced color contrast in some decorative UI elements
  • Additional keyboard shortcut documentation
  • Expanded closed captions for any future video content

ADA Compliance

We are committed to compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and its requirements as they apply to digital accessibility. We continuously review our website to identify and address accessibility barriers in accordance with:

  • Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • WCAG 2.1 Guidelines (Level AA)

Assistive Technology Support

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen Readers: JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver (macOS/iOS), TalkBack (Android)
  • Voice Recognition: Dragon NaturallySpeaking and similar tools
  • Screen Magnification: ZoomText and built-in browser/OS zoom features
  • Keyboard-Only Navigation: Full functionality without a mouse
  • High Contrast Mode: Compatible with Windows High Contrast mode

Feedback & Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of All Things Abound. If you experience accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know:

  • Email: hello@allthingsabound.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days

When contacting us about an accessibility issue, please include:

  • A description of the accessibility barrier you encountered
  • The web address (URL) where the issue occurred
  • The assistive technology you were using (if applicable)
  • Your browser and operating system

Enforcement Procedure

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility feedback, you have the right to contact the relevant enforcement body in your jurisdiction. In the United States, you may contact the U.S. Department of Justice Civil Rights Division or file a complaint under the ADA.

Formal Complaints

If you wish to file a formal accessibility complaint, you may do so by contacting us in writing at hello@allthingsabound.com. We will acknowledge receipt of your complaint within 5 business days and aim to resolve it within 30 calendar days.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We are committed to:

  • Conducting regular accessibility audits of our website
  • Training our team on accessibility best practices
  • Including accessibility requirements in our design and development processes
  • Updating this statement as we make improvements
  • Engaging with users who have disabilities to learn from their experiences

This Accessibility Statement was prepared on June 11, 2026, and will be reviewed and updated at least annually or whenever significant changes are made to the website.
