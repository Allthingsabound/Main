Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

All Things Abound is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone.

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

Our Commitment

All Things Abound is committed to making our website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to meet or exceed the requirements of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

Conformance Status

We are working toward full conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Our current website incorporates:

  • Semantic HTML5: Proper headings, landmarks, and lists for meaningful structure
  • ARIA Labels: Navigation, sections, and interactive elements include appropriate roles and labels
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible without a mouse
  • Color Contrast: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio for normal text, 3:1 for large text
  • Alt Text: All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text
  • Responsive Design: Functional across different screen sizes and orientations
  • Focus Indicators: Visible focus indicators for keyboard users
  • Text Resizing: Content remains readable and functional at up to 200% zoom
  • No Autoplay: We do not use audio or video that plays automatically

ADA Compliance

We are committed to compliance with:

  • Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
  • Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • WCAG 2.1 Guidelines (Level AA)

Assistive Technology Support

Our website is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen Readers: JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver (macOS/iOS), TalkBack (Android)
  • Voice Recognition: Dragon NaturallySpeaking and similar tools
  • Screen Magnification: ZoomText and built-in browser/OS zoom features
  • High Contrast Mode: Compatible with Windows High Contrast mode

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas may not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA. We are actively working to identify and address these, including enhanced contrast in some decorative elements and additional keyboard shortcut documentation.

Feedback & Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our site. If you experience barriers, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@allthingsabound.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 5 business days

When contacting us, please include: a description of the barrier, the URL where it occurred, the assistive technology you were using, and your browser/OS.

Formal Complaints

If you wish to file a formal accessibility complaint, contact us in writing at hello@allthingsabound.com. We will acknowledge receipt within 5 business days and aim to resolve within 30 calendar days.

Ongoing Efforts

We are committed to conducting regular accessibility audits, training our team on accessibility best practices, including accessibility requirements in our design process, and engaging with users who have disabilities to learn from their experiences. This statement will be reviewed and updated at least annually.

Need accessibility assistance?

We’re here to help. Reach out and we’ll do our best to accommodate your needs.
