1. Introduction

All Things Abound (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have questions, contact us at: hello@allthingsabound.com

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type, operating system, and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, operate, and improve our website and services; respond to inquiries; send you updates you have opted into; analyze usage patterns; and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EEA/UK users, we process data under: Consent (email subscriptions), Legitimate Interests (analytics), Contract (service delivery), and Legal Obligation (where required by law).

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with service providers who assist in operating our website (contractually bound to protect your data), legal authorities when required by law, and in connection with a business transfer with appropriate notice.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies (required for site function), analytics cookies (usage insights), and preference cookies (settings). You may control cookies through your browser. Where required by law, we obtain consent before placing non-essential cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. Email subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form data is retained for up to 2 years.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: access, correct, delete, or port your personal data; object to or restrict processing; withdraw consent; and for California residents, rights under CCPA including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale. To exercise any right, contact hello@allthingsabound.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.
